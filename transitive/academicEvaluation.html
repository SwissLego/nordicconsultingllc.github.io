Academic Evaluation and Positioning

  1. Strategic Repositioning:
    • We conduct a comprehensive assessment of academic programs, faculty, and organizational and asset infrastructure to identify strengths and areas for potential improvement.
    • Academic positioning of institutions, ensuring relevance and competitiveness in the dynamic education landscape.
    • Stakeholder Engagement: Facilitate research and conversations with institutions stakeholders to gather insights and align institutional goals.
  2. Substantive Change Management:
    • Change Readiness Assessment: Evaluate an institution's readiness for change and develop tailored change management strategies.
    • Communication Strategies: Develop effective communication plans to ensure all stakeholders understand and support the proposed changes.
    • Implementation Support: Provide hands-on support during the implementation phase, addressing challenges and ensuring a smooth transition.
  3. Accreditation Support:
    • Accreditation Readiness: Guide institutions through the preparation process for accreditation reviews, ensuring compliance with standards.
    • Documentation Assistance: Assist and accompany in the creation and organization of documentation required for accreditation/self-study submissions.
    • Continuous Improvement: Develop strategies for ongoing quality improvement to meet and exceed accreditation standards.
