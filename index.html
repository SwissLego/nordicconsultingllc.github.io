Mission statment

Charting Success in Higher Education Globally

Welcome to NORDIC Consulting LLC, your partner in navigating the dynamic landscape of international and domestic higher education. We are dedicated to charting success for educational institutions worldwide, providing a comprehensive suite of services designed to enhance academic positioning, facilitate change management, support accreditation processes, guide mergers and acquisitions, optimize enrollment and marketing strategies, offer board membership training, and evaluate institutional service profiles.

Our founder, Dr. John C. Niser, a seasoned educational visionary with a global impact, leads a team of experts ready to support you. His rich background in academic pursuits, and transformative leadership at institutions worldwide forms the foundation of our commitment to excellence.

Explore our website to learn more about our services, the visionary behind our success, and our unwavering commitment to building your success. We look forward to partnering with you on your journey to success in higher education.
